Progress Evaluation 1

The volume of the pivot / base support part and shoulder part is 645887.96 and 607363.24 mm3 respectively. Since the density of 3D printer PLA filament is 0.00124 g / mm3, the mass of the pivot / base support part and shoulder part is 800.9 and 753.13 g respectively. The length of the shoulder is 246.55 mm. The shoulder is 186.43 mm above the ground. The maximum radius of the pivot is 90 mm. The joint has mass and the claw has a significant length, which was neglected in theory. The claw will be considered a part of the elbow in this design.
